Lasagna Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna dish is made with layers of lasagna sheet and filled with bubbly sauce, cheese and vegetables

Ingredients

  • 3 onions
  • Lasagna sauce
  • courgette
  • aubergine
  • green pepper
  • salt

Method

  1. Layer pasta sheets
  2. Add cooked sauce
  3. Boil vegetables
  4. Mix with sauce
  5. Add salt
  6. Add sauce and vegetables onto layered pasta sheets
